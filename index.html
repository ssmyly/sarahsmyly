About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Emory University, specializing in health economics and Industrial Organization. My research explores the intersection of these fields, particularly structural health economics.

I hold a BS in Economics and a BA in Music from Berry College. I am a recipient of the Laney Graduate School Fellowship at Emory University.
